## Reporting an issue

When you email support, a few details help us resolve things faster:

- The MCP client you are using, for example Claude, ChatGPT, or Cursor.
- What you asked and what happened, including any error message.
- The `conversation_id` if a specific run was involved. Your assistant can read it back to you, or you can find the run in the Kepler app.

<Tip>
  Not sure whether something is a connector issue or a coverage gap? Ask Kepler to check availability first with [`lookup_company`](/mcp/tools#lookup_company). It returns instantly and confirms what filings and transcripts Kepler has on file.
</Tip>
