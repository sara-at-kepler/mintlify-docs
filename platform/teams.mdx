description: "Organizations, members, and roles in Kepler."

Kepler accounts belong to an organization, so teams can work together with access managed in one place. Admins control who is in the organization and what each member can do.

## Organizations

Your account is part of an organization that groups your team together. Work is tied to your own account, and access is scoped so members see their own conversations and results. Sign-in for the whole organization can be tied to your identity provider through [single sign-on](/sso), so adding or removing someone there controls their access to Kepler.

## Members and roles

Within an organization, members hold roles that govern what they can access and manage. Organization admins manage membership and roles from the Kepler app, so you can bring new people on and adjust access as your team changes.

## Setting up your team

For help provisioning your organization, enabling SSO, or arranging access for your team, contact your Kepler contact or email [support@kepler.ai](mailto:support@kepler.ai).
